1. Introduction

Xpert Group ("Company", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2.1 WhatsApp Business API Services

When you use our WhatsApp Business API services, we may collect:

2.2 Ecommerce Services

For our ecommerce services, we may collect:

  • Store owner contact and business information

We may automatically collect certain information when you visit our website or use our Services, including:

We use the collected information for various purposes:
